ECRI Pricing

Define the gradient and per-factor weights used to compute the per-ledger Proposed % shown on the Eligibility page.

Gradient

Score = (sum of fired positive weights / sum of all enabled positive weights)
minus the same for negatives. Clamped to ≥ 0 then linearly mapped to [Min %, Max %].

Per-Country Gradient Overrides

Optional. When set, tenants in this country use the override instead of the global gradient. Required when budgets and churn rates differ by market (e.g. SG needs a higher mean ECRI than KR).
